Stick Survival

How To Play:

For movement use your keyboard's Left and Right Arrow keys, To make the action happen just press G!
You have 5 lives, try not to lose them!(You May Be Given Extra Lives in The Game) Destroy the arrows before they hit you in order to keep your lives. The Higher The Difficulty, The Faster The Arrows!

Difficulty:

Easy Medium Hard

Weapon:

Hand Sword

Character Skin:

White Yellow

Lives Left: 5
